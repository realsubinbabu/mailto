What is a mailto link?

A mailto link is a type of HTML link that allows users to compose an email directly from a web page. When a user clicks on a mailto link, their default email client (such as Outlook or Gmail) will open with the recipient, subject, and body of the email already filled in.

Uses of a mailto link

  • Providing a way for users to contact a website's administrator or support team
  • Allowing users to easily share an email with a friend or colleague
  • Allowing users to easily send feedback or report bugs on a website
